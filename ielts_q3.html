📚 수정 이유 & 배울 표현
in my younger times → back when I was younger back when ~ = "~하던 시절"이라는 향수 어린 표현. 스토리텔링에 딱. back in the day / in my childhood
there was some atmosphere that → there used to be this culture where there used to be + this ___ where 조합은 회상형 서술의 표준. it was common for ~ to / it was part of the culture
every members of relatives → extended family members extended family가 "친척까지 포함한 대가족"의 정확한 표현. the whole extended family / all our relatives
meet and talk → get together get together = "모이다" phrasal verb. 친밀한 만남에 딱. come together / gather (as a family)
most big brother → eldest of his siblings the eldest이 "맏이" 표준 표현. brother만 쓰면 남자 형제만 포함됨. the oldest sibling / the head of the family
remember this long raws of cars → vividly remember this long line of cars trailing behind ours vividly remember + trailing behind = Band 9 감각적 묘사. this endless convoy of cars / this string of cars following us
that vibe → the atmosphere of it "vibe"은 구어체. Part 2에선 atmosphere / feel / energy가 더 세련됨. the feel of those days / that sense of togetherness
mom and aunts → my mum and my aunts 영어에선 가족 호칭 앞에 my를 붙이는 게 자연스러움. 영국식: mum / 미국식: mom (IELTS는 어느 쪽이든 OK)
preparing → grilling grill = "(직화로) 굽다" — 바비큐 장면엔 훨씬 생생. preparing은 너무 일반적. firing up the grill / manning the grill
Besides → On top of that On top of that = "게다가" — Band 9 discourse marker. What's more / To top it off
really fun → absolutely fun / a blast 강조 부사 업그레이드. 더 자연스러운 건 a blast = "엄청 재미있는 경험". an absolute blast / so much fun
can't be seen → has pretty much disappeared 수동태 "볼 수 없다"는 직역 느낌. has disappeared가 자연스럽고 감정적. is basically gone now / is rarely seen anymore
I really miss then → I really miss those days 향수 어린 마무리는 those days로. I often look back on those times / Those were the days
— 문법·표현 오류 —
every members → every member / all members every + 단수명사 규칙. every members는 항상 틀림.
used to gathered → used to gather / would gather used to + 동사원형. 반복된 과거 습관이라 would gather가 더 자연스러움.
I couldn't remember → I can't really remember couldn't는 과거 특정 시점에 못 했다는 뉘앙스. 지금도 기억 안 나면 can't remember.
raws → rows 단순 스펠링 오류. "줄"은 rows.
the number of our family was almost 60 → there were almost 60 of us "우리 가족 수"는 영어에서 there were X of us가 관용.

after meal → after the meal 특정 식사를 지칭하므로 the 필요.
Sadly, This → Sadly, this 쉼표 뒤는 소문자. 대문자 T는 오타.
even in holidays → even on holidays 특정 공휴일/명절에는 전치사 on. on Chuseok, on Christmas 같은 패턴.
