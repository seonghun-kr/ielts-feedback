Q2. Do you prefer travelling alone or with other people?
Part 1 — Travel set 1
빼거나 바꿀 부분 Band 9 개선 버전
✏️ 답안 + 수정 (통합)
I prefer traveling with others. (→ I generally prefer travelling with others.) I expect think traveling is not just about visiting another places new places, leaving or stepping out of your comfort zone. It's also about getting to know more about yourself getting to know yourself better, which is mostly happened tends to happen more naturally when with other people when you're around people. Having said that, at the same time, I think having some me time in traveling on a trip has also merits its own value in a way that in a comfort zone didn't that group travel can't offer — it gives you space to reflect. However So ideally, the best use of resources is maybe incorporating Self-traveling into traveling with others I'd combine the two: travel with others but carve out a bit of solo time along the way.
📚 수정 이유 & 배울 표현
I prefer → I generally prefer 단정적 표현에 generally를 붙이면 훨씬 세련됨. Band 9는 단호함보다 nuance. I tend to prefer / More often than not, I prefer
leaving → stepping out of step out of your comfort zone은 원어민 고정 표현. push yourself beyond / break out of
getting to know more about yourself → getting to know yourself better get to know + 사람 + better가 자연스러운 collocation. learn more about who you are / discover more about yourself
happens → tends to happen more naturally tend to = "~하는 경향이 있다" — Band 9 단골 표현. is more likely to happen / comes more easily
with other people → around people be around people = "사람들 사이에 있다". in the company of others / surrounded by others
also merits → its own value / its own merits "나름의 가치가 있다" 관용구. has its own appeal / has unique benefits
"— it gives you space to reflect" 추가 주장에 구체적 근거 한 줄 덧붙이기. Band 9의 핵심. it lets you recharge / you can truly unwind
the best use of resources → combine the two combine the two가 간결하고 명확. strike a balance / find a middle ground
incorporating A into B → carve out time carve out time = "(시간을) 일부러 만들어내다". set aside time / make room for
— 문법·표현 오류 —
expect → think expect는 "예상/기대"라 의미 안 맞음. think이나 To me,로 시작.
another places → new places another는 단수 명사만 받음. 복수엔 other/new.
is mostly happened → happens happen은 자동사라 수동태 불가능.
"Having said that" + "at the same time" 중복 둘 다 대조·전환이라 같이 쓰면 어색. 하나만!
in traveling → on a trip / while travelling "여행 중"은 on a trip 또는 while travelling.
"in a way that in a comfort zone didn't" → "that group travel can't offer" 비교 구조가 문법적으로 깨짐. 비교 대상 명확히.
However → So ideally However는 대조인데 여기선 결론. So ideally가 맞음.
Self-traveling → solo travel 콩글리시. 영어권에선 solo travel / travelling alone이 표준.
