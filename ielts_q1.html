📚 수정 이유 & 배울 표현
Well → Honestly "Well"은 생각을 끄는 느낌이고, Honestly는 솔직한 의견을 시작하는 자연스러운 opener. Band 9 답변은 첫 단어부터 자신감 있게.
I'm not sure → I have mixed feelings "확신이 안 선다"를 자연스럽게 표현하는 원어민식 숙어. mixed feelings가 훨씬 세련됨.
불필요한 filler 표현 정리 "I mean", "Because, you know", "worse still", "So, I'm not sure but" — Part 1은 간결함이 핵심. 이런 말 버릇은 점수 깎임.
a little kid → younger 형용사 앞엔 관사 X. "when I was younger"가 자연스러움 ("when I was a kid"는 OK).
did love → absolutely loved 강조할 땐 강조 부사를 붙이는 게 훨씬 자연스러움. 비슷한 패턴: totally agreed, completely forgot, really enjoyed.
needed → had "~하기만 하면 됐다"는 all I had to do가 훨씬 자연스러움. needed는 "필요했다"에 가까움.
pack it up → pack my bags up "it"이 가리키는 대상이 불명확. pack my bags가 관용 표현.
follow mom and dad → follow my parents around follow + around = "졸졸 따라다니다" 느낌의 phrasal verb. 단순 follow보다 어린이다운 그림이 그려짐.
As I grow up → As I got older (+ however → But) 과거→현재 변화는 과거시제로. "grow up"은 현재형이라 어색. 또 "however" 대신 But으로 시작하면 자연스러움.
rarely travel → have hardly travelled at all at all을 붙이면 "전혀" 강조. rarely보다 훨씬 원어민스러움. 비슷한 패턴: not at all, nothing at all.
never → never actually actually를 넣으면 "사실상 한 번도 ~해본 적 없다"는 강조 뉘앙스. Band 9 단골 표현.
it occurs to me → sometimes wonder if "가끔 ~인지 궁금하다" — sometimes wonder if가 훨씬 일상적이고 유연함.
if I didn't go trip → if I haven't gone on a trip 지속된 경험은 현재완료로. 또 "go trip"이 아니라 go on a trip이 관용구.

💡 더 나아가 모범답안에선 이 부분 자체를 간결하게 "that means I don't really enjoy it anymore"로 정리했어요. really + anymore 조합이 "예전과 달리 지금은 별로"라는 뉘앙스를 부드럽게 표현.
By the way → That said By the way는 주제 전환용, 여기선 대조가 필요하므로 That said.
worrying about nothing → without worrying about anything 이중부정은 어색. without + -ing 구조에 anything을 붙이는 게 자연스러움.
I would do travel → I would travel "do" 불필요.
readily → in a heartbeat 문맥상 in a heartbeat가 훨씬 자연스러운 표현. "주저 없이, 바로"라는 뉘앙스.
모범 답안 전체 흐름 "Honestly, I have mixed feelings about it. When I was younger, I absolutely loved travelling — all I had to do was pack my bags and follow my parents around. But as I got older, I've hardly travelled at all, and I've never actually planned a trip on my own. So I sometimes wonder if that means I don't really enjoy it anymore. That said, if I had enough time and money to travel the world without worrying about anything, I'd do it in a heartbeat."
