📚 수정 이유 & 배울 표현
Well → Honestly 솔직한 의견을 시작하는 자연스러운 opener. Well은 망설이는 인상. To be honest / Frankly
I'm not sure → I have mixed feelings mixed feelings가 훨씬 세련됨. I'm a bit torn / I'm on the fence
a little kid → younger 형용사 앞엔 관사 X. "when I was younger"가 자연스러움. when I was a kid / in my childhood
did love → absolutely loved 감정 강도는 강조 부사로. Band 9 단골 패턴. totally loved / completely adored
needed → had "~하기만 하면 됐다"는 all I had to do가 자연스러움.
follow mom and dad → follow my parents around follow + around = "졸졸 따라다니다" phrasal verb. 어린이다운 그림. tag along with my parents / trail after them
rarely travel → have hardly travelled at all at all이 "전혀"를 강조. rarely보다 원어민스러움. barely travelled / haven't travelled much
never → never actually actually로 "사실상 한 번도 ~해본 적 없다" 강조.
it occurs to me → sometimes wonder if sometimes wonder if가 훨씬 일상적이고 유연함. I sometimes ask myself whether / I start to question if
I don't like trips → I don't really enjoy it anymore really + anymore = "예전과 달리 지금은 별로"라는 부드러운 부정.
By the way → That said By the way는 주제 전환용. 여기선 대조라 That said. Having said that / Still,
readily → in a heartbeat in a heartbeat = "주저 없이, 바로" 원어민 숙어. without hesitation / at the drop of a hat
— 문법·표현 오류 —
I mean / Because, you know / worse still / So, I'm not sure but 불필요한 filler 표현. Part 1은 간결함이 핵심.
pack it up → pack my bags up "it"이 가리키는 대상이 불명확. pack my bags가 관용 표현.
As I grow up → As I got older 과거→현재 변화는 과거시제. "grow up"은 현재형이라 어색.
if I didn't go trip → if I haven't gone on a trip 지속된 경험은 현재완료. "go trip"이 아니라 go on a trip.
worrying about nothing → without worrying about anything 이중부정은 어색. without + -ing + anything.
I would do travel → I would travel "do" 불필요.
